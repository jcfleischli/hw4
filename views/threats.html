Types of cybersecurity threats

Threats can come from both the software side and through social engineering. Here are some of the most common cyberthreats.

  • Malware - maliciously designed software that is harmful to a computer user.
  • Ransomware - a type of malware that blocks parts of the computer off to the user until a set ransom is paid.
  • Social Engineering - planned manipulation of users who are being tricked to grant attackers access to otherwise confidential or sensitive data.
  • Phishing - fradulant forms of communication are sent from what looks like reputable sources. These are sent with the attentiuon to steal important data.
