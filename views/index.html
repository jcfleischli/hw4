What is cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is what protects hardware, software and data from cyber attacks. Similar to physical security, the goal of cybersecurity is to create a stronger defense against attackers trying to breach their data or other assets. Cybersecurity is in place to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of private data.

As our professional and personal lives continue to grow dependence on the internet and digitilization of data, it becomes more of a necessity to put an emphasise on cybersecurity. All businesses who maintain or process customer data should have cybersecurity as a main priority to protect from potential breaches.
